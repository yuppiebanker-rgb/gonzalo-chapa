Gonzalo Chapa is a photographer based in San Pedro Garza García, Monterrey. His work documents the energy of northern México — from the masked characters of its streets to the neon-lit underground of its nightlife and the raw landscape of the Sierra Madre.

His practice is built on trust, patience, and a refusal to pose the world. Whether documenting a concert, shooting a portrait, or walking a carretera at dusk, he brings the same editorial eye: honest, bold, and rooted in place.
